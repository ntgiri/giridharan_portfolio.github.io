Featured Project

Operating Room Etiquette and Safety

The business goal
R&D engineers at a medical device company need to conduct live observations in operating rooms. When researchers are aware of the end user’s needs, they can design medical devices that are more effective.

Why aren’t they doing it?
Surgical teams operate under strict protocol and many other regulations. The research team must be aware of these rules to gain access to the OR.

The solution
This E-learning course provides a scenario-based simulation for researchers to identify and avoid unsafe practices in an OR.
