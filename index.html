Operating Room Etiquette and Safety

R&D engineers at a medical device company are required to conduct live observations in operating rooms. These visits will help the engineers identify real-world problems that surgeons face in their daily work. However, surgical teams operate under strict protocol and many other regulations. How can the team learn some of these rules and gain access to the OR? This E-learning course provides a scenario-based simulation for researchers to identify and avoid unsafe practices in an OR.
